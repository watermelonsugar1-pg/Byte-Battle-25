Common Types of Scams

  • OTP Scams: Asking for verification codes.
  • Lottery Scams: Messages claiming prizes requiring payment.
  • Fake Job Scams: High salary offers asking for registration fees.
  • Tech Support Scams: Fake calls about hacked devices.
  • Phishing Links: Fake websites stealing passwords.

Hero AI Abilities

  • Scan Mode: Detects suspicious messages.
  • Firewall Lift: Protects your data.
  • Identity Tracker: Finds cloned numbers.
  • Auto-Warning: Warns before risky clicks.
  • b Reprogram Beam: Converts bad bots to helpers.

Safety Steps

  1. Never share OTPs or passwords.
  2. Stay calm when receiving scary messages.
  3. Check official sources before trusting messages.
  4. Use strong passwords and 2-factor authentication.
  5. Keep your phone and apps updated.

How to Report Scams

  • National Cybercrime Portal: cybercrime.gov.in
  • Helpline: 1930 (India)
  • Local Police: File complaints.
  • Bank Helplines for financial fraud.
