About the Game

A tiny hero AI battles corrupted scam-bots in a neon digital city, rescuing people from fake calls and messages. In the final boss fight, the hero shatters the giant Corrupted AI, exposes the humans running the scams on every screen, and reprograms all the bad bots into friendly guardians that warn everyone about future scams.
